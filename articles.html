How to buy CRP using a bank card on Latoken ?

*Note: It is currently impossible to buy coins on Latoken using fiat if you are a resident/citizen of the USA.

Step 1: Register on the exchange and buy digital dollars

  • If you are not registered on the exchange, the first step is to register on the Latoken exchange;
  • Since the CPR is traded in a pair with the digital dollar, we need to first transfer our Fiat money to USDT (equivalent to dollars);
  • Click on “Deposit” in the upper right corner;
  • Select the currency that you want to buy;
  • In the drop-down list, select USDT and click buy.

*Note: Keep in mind that buying coins may take time: your account will be credited after 15 network confirmations. Min deposit is 3 USDT. Lower amount will not be credited. Deposit Fee is 0%. You can check current transaction status on the transactions tab.

  • Fill in the amount you would like to purchase. (Make sure you read and understood the instructions first);
  • Select the currency to buy USDT. The system will then offer you a list of banks and services through which you can buy USDT crypto dollars. An individual plan is selected for each region;
  • Click on the Bank through which the purchase will take place.
  • Complete the form and enter your Card details.

*Note: All data is conducted not through the exchange but the Bank. All transactions are reflected, so everything is safe in double measure.

  • Once you’re done filling your personal details, billing address, click ‘Continue’. The system will move you to the payment where you can introduce your card details.

*Note: As soon as you finish the final stage and your transaction is confirmed in the blockchain, you’ll see your USDT tokens in your LATOKEN wallet.

Step 2: How to buy CRP tokens

Now that you have USDT, you can easily buy a CRP coin. For this, follow these steps:

  • Click on the “Exchange” button in the menu at the top of the screen;
  • You will be redirected to a page where you can choose a cryptocurrency to buy;
  • Enter CRP in the search bar and the system will automatically find Crypton;
  • Next, click on the “Trade” button;
  • Buy CRP on the exchange. For this, follow the instruction:
    • Choose the purchase price of the coins. This can be done simply by clicking on the price offered on the market (indicated by red numbers);
    • Select the number of coins to buy;
    • Confirm the action and click “Buy”;

Note: Don’t forget about the number of coins offered at each price. On the screen, you see that for $0.18 you can buy only 100 coins. If you want more, you need to choose a different value and make a purchase.

Now all your Cryptons on a cryptocurrency exchange Latoken’s wallet.

How to withdraw CRP from the exchange for storage on your personal Utopia P2P wallet? Read the full instruction here.

If you want to withdraw coins from a crypto wallet, you can read the next article.

How to buy CRP on ZG.com ?

How to Deposit Crypton on ZG Exchange ?

  • Create your account on ZG.com if you don’t have one already;
  • Log in to your account and go to Balance > Wallet;
  • In the “Search Coins” box, type “CRP” or “Crypton” and you will see that Utopia’s Crypton will appear with it’s a distinctive diamond icon;
  • Click on “Deposit” which you will see in front of CRP;
  • A new deposit page will open with a Deposit Address and a TAG. Both of them are very important and you will need them to deposit your Crypton from uWallet to ZG exchange;

Once you have setup ZG and you have the required information, let’s head over to Utopia client to proceed with the deposit:

  • Open uWallet inside “Utopia Client” and click on “Send Cryptons” (you can also click on ‘Send’ which appears below your CRP balance);
  • Select from the drop down menu any crypto card or your uWallet to send CRP from;
  • Enter the “Deposit Address” and paste it in the “Send to:” field;
  • Enter the ‘Tag‘ and paste it in “Comment:” field;

*Note: You must copy the Deposit address / Tag and paste as mentioned above in an exact manner. (Your tag number will vary while deposit address can change in the future as well.)

How to Deposit Crypton on Latoken

  • Log in to your LATOKEN account via https://latoken.com/;
  • Click on the “Wallet tab”, then on the “Deposit” button next to the “CRP” coin.
    If you do not see the CRP in the list, just enter ticker “CRP” in the search box.

*Note: To quickly get the CRP deposit address for your account, click on “Copy”, and paste the deposit address to the corresponding platform’s address section (your Utopia’s wallet) intend to withdraw your funds from.

  • A new deposit page will open with a Deposit Address. Also, there is an unimportant field “Memo” that you can leave empty.

Once you have setup Latoken and you have the required information, let’s head over to Utopia client to proceed with the deposit:

  • Open uWallet inside “Utopia Client” and click on “Send Cryptons” (you can also click on ‘Send’ which appears below your CRP balance);
  • Select from the drop down menu any crypto card or your uWallet to send CRP from;
  • Enter the “Deposit Address” and paste it in the “Send to:” field.

How to store Cryptons in your wallet?

You can keep your purchased tokens on the exchange.

However, if you are an investor and will not trade your crypto assets, the best option is to store the CPR on your wallet and not on the exchange.

Step 1: How to create a wallet for Cryptons?

If you are not a Utopia P2P user yet, then you need to:

  • Follow the link and download the ecosystem: https://u.is/en/download.html;
  • Set and create a password. After that, the installation will be performed.

Now, you are a client of the Utopia P2P Ecosystem. You have access to your personal wallet and all the functions of the ecosystem.

*Note: Avoid an awkward situation and don’t rely on memory. Save the created username and password on multiple media devices.

  • Find out the address for storing coins. Click on File>My profile.
  • Look at your encrypted address (Public key). It is your address for sending coins.
  • All your coins will be stored in your Utopia P2P wallet. Click uWallet > Open uWallet. Or click on the diamond icon.

Step 2: How to Withdraw Crypton from ZG Exchange?

  • Open ZG Exchange and browse to your Exchange Wallet ( Balance > Wallet );
  • If you have deposited any Crypton you will see CRP among the top of the list, you can always check “hide small balances” to hide all those 0 balance wallets to make your wallet look tidy;
  • Click on “Withdraw” in front of CRP Coin;
  • In Withdrawal Address field add your Public Key, as that is your Wallet address;
  • Turn on the “No Tag” toggle as Tag is not required while withdrawing Crypton;
  • Click on Withdraw after verifying everything. In few moments you will get your CRP in your Client uWallet.

After confirmation in the blockchain, approximately 30-40 minutes, your coins will be in the P2P wallet.

Step 3: How to Withdraw Crypton from Latoken?

  • Open your account on https://exchange.latoken.com/;
  • Go to the “Wallet tab” and select CRP to withdraw - “Send” button
  • If you make a withdrawal for the first time, the system will ask you to enter and confirm your phone number to protect you from your funds' theft

*Note: Don’t use the email address option if you want to withdraw your funds.

  • See the minimum amount of funds you need to withdraw, as well as the withdrawal fee;
  • Copy the “CRP Deposit Address" from the corresponding platform (Utopia P2P wallet) or wallet you want to withdraw your funds to and paste it to the “Address” section;
  • Input the amount of that asset that you want to withdraw. The system will add the transaction fee, and calculate the amount you will get on the corresponding platform or wallet;
  • Leave the "Memo" field empty.

*Note: Please, be kindly advised.

Withdrawal from LATOKEN is a deposit action to the corresponding platform (your Utopia p2p ecosystem’s wallet).

How to start to play online chess in Utopia

1. Download Utopia P2P ecosystem or update your exciting client account.

2. Find out on the panel the “Tools” option.

3. Click on the “Games” button.

4. Choose the “Chess” game.

5. Select your game opponent from your contact list.

If your contact book doesn’t include the contact of the desired person, you can add him by hand. For that you should follow these steps and open a special channel with all chess lovers:

  • Find out Open Channel Manager option.
  • Enter Channel ID (07472EAE6968FDBE0B6683C5729327AB) in the search line.

  • Click on the Utopia Chess channel to find a game opponent.
  • Click on the desired user.
  • Click on “Ask user’s Public Key”

*Note: You should notice your competitor about the desire to play chess with him in advance. The simple way for that is to send a message in the chat.

If the user want to play with you, he will confirm the request and send you this information. Then you need to copy and add his Public Key to your contact list:

  • Find out on the panel the “Contacts” option.
  • Click on the “Add contacts” button.
  • Insert in the search line a Public Key or uCode of the desired person.

6. If the user want to play with you, he will confirm the request.

Now, your chessboard are opened and you can play online chess with your friend. If you don’t know how to start, let’s follow the next steps:

1. You need to move the chess piece to the selected location.

2. The ecosystem provides hints on where to make the best move.

3. The name of the player who should move is written in bold.

4. All won pieces are located in captured pieces field.

5. If you want to message your opponent, you can write a message in built-in instant chat.

How to start mining?

1. Download Utopia P2P ecosystem and get your private key. You will need it to point out the mining bot where to store your Cryptons. Your account gets a private wallet that will hold all of your minted coins.

2. Download Utopia Mining Bot

Mining Bot for Linux (RPM) Mining Bot for Linux (DEB)
  • Setup the Utopia Mining Bot on a virtual private server VPS with Linux OS;
  • Connect to the VPS through SSH (use PuTTY app for this);
  • After installation, you need to run the app;
  • Enter your VPS IP address in the host name field;
  • Leave the default setting port 22 and connection type of SSH and click open;
  • Get a security alert about a security key;
  • Click “Yes” to add the new server’s key;
  • Connect login as root and copy the password you’ve received from the VPS provider into the clipboard;
  • Paste it into the password field of the PuTTY SSH session (you can do this by right-clicking a single time or by entering your password manually);
  • When you’ve logged in successfully, you should follow the commands as demonstrated in the video above;
  • Then, open your Utopia account;
  • Copy the public key of your master;
  • Congratulations. Your mining bot is successfully started.

*Note: It may take several hours for your mining bot to establish enough peer connections to become active on the network, and begin earning Crypton rewards in the uWallet of your master client.

Follow the next commands to run Utopia Alternative Miner in Linux:

1. sudo apt update

2. wget https://update.u.is/downloads/ uam/linux/uam-latest_amd64.deb

3. sudo dpkg -i uam-latest_amd64.deb

4. cd /opt/uam

5. ./uam --pk YOUR PUBLIC KEY HERE

6. Utopia Mining Bot will start. Done!
